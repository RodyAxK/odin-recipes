Burger

Description

No more dry, lackluster hamburgers. These are juicy, and spices can be easily added or changed to suit anyone's taste.
If you find the meat mixture too mushy, just add more bread crumbs until it forms patties that hold their shape.

ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Egg
  • Dry Bread Crumbs
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Worchester Sauce
  • Seaosonings
  • Minced Garlic

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the grill for high heat.
  3. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl with your hands.
  4. Form the mixture into paties.
  5. Grill the paties.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
