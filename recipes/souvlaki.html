Souvlaki

Description

Souvlaki is a Greek specialty made with tender cuts of meat.
In this recipe, pork tenderloin is marinated in a lemony mixture of olive oil and oregano.
Serve with rice pilaf and a Greek salad.

ingredients

  • Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Lemon Juice
  • Seaosonings
  • Pork
  • Vegetables
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. Make the marinade.
  2. Marinate the ingredients in the fridge for two to three hours.
  3. Thread the marinated ingredients onto the skewers.
  4. Grill until the pork is no longer pink in the center.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
